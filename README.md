# Personal README

## What is This?

This is a **personal user manual** — a guide to help people understand how to work effectively with me. Think of it as documentation for collaborating with a human.

### Why Have a Personal README?

Working with people is complex. We all have different communication styles, working preferences, and things that energise or frustrate us. A personal README helps:

- **Reduce friction** — Teammates learn your preferences upfront rather than through trial and error
- **Build trust faster** — Transparency about your quirks and growth areas shows self-awareness and vulnerability
- **Save time** — Clearer communication expectations mean fewer misunderstandings
- **Improve collaboration** — When people know what makes you tick, they can work with you more effectively
- **Normalise feedback** — Stating how you prefer feedback makes it easier for others to give it

### How This Helps Teams Work Better Together

When everyone on a team shares their personal README, it creates a culture of openness and mutual understanding. You can:

- Understand how to communicate with different teammates (direct vs. indirect, written vs. verbal)
- Know when people are most productive and available
- Learn what each person's strengths are and when to tap into them
- Avoid common frustrations by understanding triggers upfront
- Give feedback in ways that land well with each individual

This isn't about making excuses for behavior — it's about creating shared context that helps everyone do their best work together.
